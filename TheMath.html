Month 1

In the first month I would create a plan to spend my money the most efficiently. To set it up, I am going to college and I am living with my parents while paying rent. I own a car that I use to drive to my college and am mostly fed by my parents. I pay a phone bill. I buy my own food. I pay for my own gas.

To start my utilities and rent is $400, My phone bill is $80, Food is around $80 and I pay around $30 to $40 for gas. With this budget I can easily make it through the first month with only spending around $580.
